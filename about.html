About Us

Stitches Unlimited is a company based out of Humboldt that provides embroidery tools at an affordable price. We have been running well for 25 years and are excited for the future. We offer a huge selection of promotional items that can be fully customized with YOUR logo! We supply uniforms, workwear, casual wear, and all types of apparel as well as daily objects including lanyards, patches, bookbags, pins, flashlights, candy, etc. Our business works with over 3000 suppliers to make sure your product is professionally and efficiently produced with care. Here at Stitches Unlimited, our goal is to help you create long-lasting relationships with your clients through the power of promotional products.
